# CYBERSEC 2026 Optimized Deck Narrative Plan

Audience: CYBERSEC 2026 healthcare security, medical-device, regulatory, QA, product, and security engineering attendees.

Objective: deliver a 30-minute conference talk that reframes AI SaMD security from model-only security into a product trust system: scope the product, trace evidence, test exposure, govern findings, and prove repairability.

Narrative arc:

1. Break the myth: `你賣的不是模型`.
2. Show urgency: AI becomes infrastructure, incidents become care disruption.
3. Install the map: four product scales determine risk and evidence burden.
4. Translate regulation: FDA 524B and FDA 2025 become an evidence chain.
5. Localize governance: model evidence, governance language, and AI-stack security.
6. Apply the map: compare early and advanced product scales.
7. Land the patient-safety peak: `Cyber Safety Is Patient Safety`.
8. Convert testing into operation: findings require Patch SLA decisions.
9. Close with action: 30 / 60 / 90, then trust before audit.

Slide list: the deck uses the 14-slide compact structure from `data/presentation_os.json`. Slide 2 is the required CYBERSEC disclaimer image when available.

Visual system: dark high-contrast base, one primary cyan accent, one red risk color, large editable text, diagram-led slides, no decorative hacker or AI-brain imagery, no screenshot-only slides.

Editability plan: all slide titles, labels, process nodes, roadmap steps, and speaker notes are editable PowerPoint text and shapes. The only image-based slide is the required CYBERSEC disclaimer source image.
